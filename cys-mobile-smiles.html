About Us

Compassionate Dental Care, On Your Terms


Hi, I'm Dr. Cyp — a licensed dentist with over 4 years of experience in preventive and restorative care. I am passionate about serving the underserved, including seniors, children, individuals battling substance abuse, and those living with physical disabilities.

We believe everyone deserves access to quality dental care, no matter their circumstances. Our fully equipped mobile unit brings comprehensive services directly to you — at home, at a care facility, school, or wherever you need us. We also offer same-day emergency visits for established patients.
